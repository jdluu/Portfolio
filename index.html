About

Hi, I'm Jeffrey Luu! I'm currently a 3rd year student studying Human Computer Interaction with a minor in Computer Science at UCSD, set to graduate in June 2023.

Ever since I was young, I immersed myself into the world of technology, constantly tinkering with my computer and learning new things. I also found joy in volunteering spending 5 years at the same food bank helping every weekend.

Now, I'm working towards bridging the intersection between technology and human interaction, making more functional and accesssible web and mobile interfaces for everyone.

Projects

ZooSeeker

ZooSeeker is an Android application that allows for users to plan out a day at the San Diego Zoo, complete with an optimized path through their selected exhibits.

Learning From Health

Learning from Health is a data science project, utilizing data from the CDC to create predictive models for income based on statistics on health conditions like diabetes, cholesterol, kidney disease, etc.

UrBenefit

An accessible platform/social application for crowdsourcing volunteers for humanitarian and art-based community efforts.

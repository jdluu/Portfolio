How to make egg-fried rice!

  1. Beat 2 eggs and mix them into some leftover rice(leave overnight).
  2. Using vegetable oil, oil the pan(a wok is better).
  3. Pour in your rice, moving around the rice occasionally.
  4. (Optional)Add in any meat or vegetables that you have.
  5. Enjoy!
